Frequently asked questions

What is web hosting and who hosts the website?

Web hosting is a process that enables and keeps websites go live in the World Wide Web and makes the websites accessible by users. A web hosting company or web host can host websites for their clients. For hosting websites, the web hosting company must have one or more servers acting as host, colocation( renting bandwidth, space, computing services etc) for the servers, a web server, and a Domain Name System. Not every company can provide web hosting as owning a server is costly and burdensome.

Will my website be mobile friendly?

We ensure to create responsive websites that are accessible on all types of devices be it android, apple, or PCs. Responsive websites are websites that can be easily accessible by any device and through all operating systems. Responsive web designs automatically adjust the website’s orientation for various screens and provide the optimum look and feel for ease of access from the user end.

How does support work?

We're aware of the importance of well qualified support, that is why we decided that support will only be provided by the authors that actually worked on this project.

Feel free to contact us and we'll help you out as soon as we can.

How can I generate leads from my website?

Websites are the best way to generate leads without even physically approaching. By creating database forms for the user and using email signups it becomes easy to get leads to reach the potential customers. It is obvious that whoever signs up at your website is interested for the service or product you provide. Therefore the leads you get from your website are surely your potential customers who can be easily converted into your service buyers.

Will you help me redesign my existing website?

Yes, we can redesign an existing website. It might take some time depending upon the existing design and your need. As redesigning is not building a website from scratch, it is a bit challenging but obviously doable thing. Redesigning also helps a website get better rank than previous eventually. A website with lower rank will obviously need a redesign and a revamped CMS to thrive on SERPs.

Is it possible for you to handle ongoing maintenance for a website?

Website maintenance includes keeping up the website updated, update necessary information continuously, hosting, and backend maintenance. If your website is built by us, it is obvious that we are bound to provide website maintenance for you. But, if it is an existing website, then taking the charge of maintenance might be challenging. However, we can surely take charge of the ongoing maintenance of your existing website.

A dedicated developer will take care of every aspect of designing, database, maintenance, and bug fixes.
